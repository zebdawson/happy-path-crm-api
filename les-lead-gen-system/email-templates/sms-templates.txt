📋 GENERAL SMS BEST PRACTICES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DO:
✓ Get explicit opt-in consent before sending SMS
✓ Personalize with name and relevant details
✓ Provide immediate value in every message
✓ Make it conversational (like a friend texting)
✓ Include clear call-to-action
✓ Respond quickly to replies (within 15 minutes if possible)
✓ Use emojis sparingly for visual interest
✓ Time messages appropriately (9am-7pm)

DON'T:
✗ Send more than 1 message per day
✗ Use ALL CAPS (seems spammy)
✗ Send generic, templated-sounding messages
✗ Ignore replies
✗ Send during early morning or late night
✗ Be too salesy - provide value first
✗ Forget the opt-out option

📊 TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Key metrics to track:
- Delivery rate (should be 95%+)
- Open rate (SMS is typically 98%+)
- Response rate (aim for 20-30%)
- Click-through rate on links (15-25%)
- Opt-out rate (keep under 2%)
- Conversion to appointment (measure by service)

A/B testing ideas:
- Time of day (morning vs afternoon)
- Message length (short vs detailed)
- CTA type (call vs text vs click)
- Emoji use (with vs without)
- Personalization level (name only vs specific details)

⚖️ COMPLIANCE & LEGAL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TCPA Compliance (Telephone Consumer Protection Act):
- Must have written consent to send marketing SMS
- Include opt-out instructions in every message
- Honor opt-out requests immediately (within 24 hours)
- Keep records of consent
- Don't send to numbers on Do Not Call registry (for cold outreach)

Standard Opt-Out Language:
"Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg&data rates may apply."

Privacy Statement (include in form submissions):
"By submitting this form, you consent to receive SMS messages from {COMPANY}.
Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out. Privacy policy: {URL}"
